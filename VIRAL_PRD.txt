egg Product Requirements Document 
Connect Brands with UGC Creators

Platform: Web 
Audience: Project Owners, Marketing Agencies, UGC Creators 


1. Project Overview 
1.1 Concept 
egg is a Tunisian platform that connects project owners and marketing agencies with UGC (User-Generated Content) creators. Instead of a traditional product shop, this is a talent/service marketplace — clients browse, filter, and hire content creators who produce authentic content for their brands. The platform launches in Tunisia and uses the Tunisian Dinar (DT) as its base currency. 
1.2 Key Value Proposition 
• Brands discover and hire vetted UGC creators quickly and securely 
• Creators gain visibility and paid opportunities based on their performance and activity on the platform 
• A coin-based economy (1 DT = 10 Coins) ensures fair, trackable transactions entirely within the app 
• Ratings, reviews, and verification build trust between all parties 

1.3 Target Users

Client / Buyer 		Project owners, marketing agencies, brand managers 	Find and hire creators for content campaigns 
Creator / Seller 	UGC creators, influencers, content producers 		Get discovered, get hired, get paid in real money 
Admin / Support 	Platform operators and support team 			Verify profiles, manage reviews, handle disputes, monitor transactions 



2. Coin Economy System 
The platform uses an internal virtual currency called Coins. All transactions happen in coins inside the app. Real Tunisian Dinar (DT) is converted to coins on entry, and creators can withdraw their earned coins as real DT on demand. 

2.1 Exchange Rate 
Fixed Exchange Rate 1 DT (Tunisian Dinar) = 10 Coins. This rate applies to both purchases by clients and withdrawals by creators.

2.2 Client Coin Wallet 
• Clients buy coins by topping up their wallet with real DT 
• Coins are deducted automatically for: profile visits, premium section access, and creator payments 
• Wallet balance always visible in the app header/nav bar 
• Low balance notification sent when coins drop below a threshold 
• Each time a client visits creator profile, 1 coin is deducted from the client's wallet

Basic 		10 DT 		110 Coins 		+10 bonus 
Pro 		25 DT 		290 Coins 		+40 bonus
Agency 		50 DT 		620 Coins 		+120 bonus

Note: Coin packages and bonus amounts can be adjusted at any time by the Admin. 										
										
										
2.3 Creator Coin Wallet 
• Creators also have a coin wallet — they must maintain a coin balance to remain visible on the platform 
• Creators top up their wallet with real DT (same 1 DT = 10 Coins rate) 
• If the creator's wallet reaches 0 coins, their profile is automatically hidden from search and the discovery feed 
• When a creator completes an order and receives coins from a client, those coins are added to their wallet balance 



2.4 Creator Withdrawal (Versement) 
Creators can request a withdrawal of their coin balance at any time, converting coins back to real DT. 
Platform Commission 
When a creator requests a withdrawal, the platform deducts 10% of the total amount as a service commission. The creator receives 90% of their coin balance in DT. 
Example calculation displayed on the creator's wallet screen: 

Field 				Example Value 
Coin Balance 			500 Coins 
Gross Value (in DT) 		50 DT (at 1 DT = 10 coins) 
Platform Commission (10%) 	- 5 DT 
Net Payout (real DT received) 	45 DT 


• The creator sees both figures clearly: Gross Amount and Net Amount After Commission 
• Withdrawal is processed by the admin/support team and paid via bank transfer or mobile payment 
• Withdrawal history is visible in the creator's account under 'My Earnings' 




3.1 Client Flow 
1. Client opens the app and sees a feed of creator profiles (contact details locked) 
2. Client registers or logs in 
3. Client purchases coins to load their wallet (pays in DT) 
4. Client browses and filters creators using the filter panel 
5. Client clicks a creator profile — if the creator is Available, 1 coin is deducted from the client's wallet for the visit (only on first visit per creator) 
6. Client reads the full profile: bio, portfolio, stats, reviews, and prices 
7. Client builds a custom order using the interactive price configurator 
8. Client contacts the creator or phone number (visible on profile) 
9. Client pays the creator using coins — coins transfer from client wallet to creator wallet 
10. Client leaves a star rating and review after the project is completed 

3. User Flows 
3.2 Creator Flow 
11. Creator registers and submits their profile for admin verification 
12. Creator fills in: niche, bio, platforms, prices, portfolio videos/photos, availability 
13. Admin verifies the profile — once approved, the creator appears in search 
14. Creator tops up their coin wallet (must maintain a positive balance to remain visible) 
15. Creator is discovered by clients
16. Creator receives messages and orders
17. Creator toggles availability status (Available / Unavailable with a return date) 
18. Creator completes work and delivers content to the client 
19. Creator receives coins as payment — visible in their wallet 
20. Creator requests a withdrawal — receives net DT after 10% platform commission

4.5 Creator Availability Toggle 
The creator has a dedicated toggle in their dashboard to manage their availability status. 
• Toggle options: Available or Unavailable 
• When setting to Unavailable, the creator can optionally enter a return date (e.g., 'Back on 15 August 2025') 
• When Unavailable: the creator's profile is hidden from the discovery feed and search results 
• When Unavailable: the creator's wallet is NOT charged coins since no new visits occur 
• The creator's profile remains accessible to clients who had previously visited it 
• When the return date is reached, the creator receives a notification to reactivate their profile 
• The creator can manually switch back to Available at any time before the return date 


4.7 Review & Rating System 
• After a completed order, the client can leave a review: star rating (1-5) and written comment 
• Overall star rating on the profile is the calculated average of all reviews 
• Reviews are permanent — neither the client nor the creator can delete them 
• To remove a review, a support request must be submitted to the admin team 
• Admins review each request individually — a review is only deleted if it is confirmed to be fake, abusive, or violating platform rules 
• Reviews are sorted by most recent by default 


5. Admin & Support Team The admin and support team manage the platform integrity, verify creators, handle disputes, and oversee the financial flow. 

5.1 Creator Profile Verification Before a creator appears on the platform, their profile must be verified by the admin/support team. 
• Creator submits their profile with: photo, bio, platforms (with links), niche, prices, and portfolio 
• Admin reviews the submission and checks: real social media accounts, content quality, prices set correctly, no inappropriate content 
• Admin can: Approve (profile goes live), Request Changes (send feedback to creator), or Reject (profile blocked) 
• Creator receives a notification with the result of the verification 
• Any major profile update (new prices, new portfolio) may trigger a re-verification step 

5.2 Review Management 
• Admin receives review removal requests from clients or creators 
• Admin investigates the request — checks if the review is fake, fabricated, or abusive 
• Admin can permanently delete a review if justified 
• Both parties are notified of the outcome 
• Admin can see the full review history for any creator 

5.3 Financial & Withdrawal Management 
• Admin sees all coin purchase transactions (which client, amount, date) 
• Admin receives creator withdrawal requests 
• Admin confirms the net payout (after 10% commission) and processes the DT transfer 
• Admin marks the withdrawal as 'Processed' — creator is notified 
• Admin can see the full earnings and withdrawal history per creator 

5.4 Other Admin Capabilities 
• View and manage all user accounts (suspend, ban, reactivate) 
• Manage coin packages: add, edit, or remove pricing tiers 
• Promote or remove creators from the Premium tier 
• Access platform analytics: active users, revenue, top creators, most visited profiles 
• Handle support tickets and resolve disputes between clients and creators
